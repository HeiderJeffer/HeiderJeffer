An in-depth, self-directed exploration of the 11-volume *Story of
Civilization* series, focusing on historical, philosophical, and
cultural evolution through detailed summaries, critical analysis, and
personal reflection.

**Key Contributions**

- Explored patterns in societal and cultural development

### Participatory Modeling for Societal Complexity in Healthcare

*Research Lead: Heider Jeffer*  
Developed and led a research initiative using participatory modeling to
address complex stakeholder dynamics in healthcare systems. Employed
interactive simulations and probabilistic models (Markov Chains, HMMs)
to explore engagement strategies and decision pathways.

- Designed and deployed dynamic stakeholder simulation tools:

  - [Dynamic Stakeholder
    Simulation](https://github.com/HeiderJeffer/Modeling-for-Societal-Complexity-in-Healthcare/blob/main/dynamic_stakeholder_simulation.ipynb)

  - [Hidden Markov Model Stakeholder
    Simulation](https://github.com/HeiderJeffer/Modeling-for-Societal-Complexity-in-Healthcare/blob/main/hmm_stakeholder_simulation.ipynb)

  - [Markov Chain Stakeholder
    Engagement](https://github.com/HeiderJeffer/Modeling-for-Societal-Complexity-in-Healthcare/blob/main/markov_chain_stakeholder_engagement.ipynb)

- Enabled real-time analysis of stakeholder behavior and engagement
  patterns

- Released tools through GitHub and Google Colab with a rolling update
  model

- Supported by comprehensive research documentation and long-term
  engagement data
